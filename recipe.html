AFRICA SALAD RECIPE
A guide to cooking an africa salad has been made simple. just follow along.


Ingredients
  • 3 handfuls abacha
  • 2 cups ugba (ukpaka)
  • 30 cl red palm oil
  • 2 tablespoons powdered potash (food tenderizer)
  • Fish | mackerel/dry fish/stockfish
  • 1 large onions
  • salt and dry pepper (to taste)
  • 4 tablespoons ground crayfish
  • 2 stock cubes (maggi/knorr)
  • 1 teaspoons ground ehu seeds(calabash nutmeg)
